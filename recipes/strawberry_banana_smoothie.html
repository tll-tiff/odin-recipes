StrawBan Smoothie Recipe

Description

A refreshing strawberry banana smoothie that's a little sweet, a little creamy, and extremely good.


Ingredients

Servings: 2 to 3

  • 1 1/2 cup raspberries
  • 1 cup strawberries
  • 1/2 frozen banana
  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 1 1/2 cups of ice
  • 2 scoops of protein powder of your choice
  • Optional: Mint or Basil

Instructions

  1. Pour milk into blender.
  2. Put protein powder in.
  3. Add in rest of ingredients in any order.
  4. Blend:Brrrrrrrrrr
  5. Enjoy!
