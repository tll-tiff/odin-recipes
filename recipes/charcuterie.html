Charcuterie Board Recipe


Description

Charcuterie which translates to "pork-butcher shop" from French,is known for it's assemblance of crackers, cheeses, fruits, meats, nuts, spreads, and anything else you want to add! Pairs well with wine ; )

Recipe

Tools

  • Serving Board
  • Cheese Knives
  • Small Dishes: for spreads, jams, olives, pickles, etc.

Ingredients

    Meats
    • Peppered Salami
    • Deli Hams
    • Proscuitto
    Cheeses
    • Boursin Dairy-Free Garlic-Herb Cheese
    • Softened Brie
    • Camembert
    Fruits and Nuts
    • Grapes
    • Raisins
    • Strawberries
    • Cherries
    • Pistachios
    • Almonds
    • Peanuts
    Crackers and Bread
    • Pretzels
    • Crackers

Instructions

  1. Create A Theme:
    • What flavors do you want to focus on?
    • How are you going to serve it?
    • Are you making this board for a specific holiday or occasion?
  2. Check Your Pantry and Fridge: You probably have some ingredients already!
  3. Arrange Ingredients: You can use different colors and textures to complement each other.
  4. Balance Out Your Board: If you have a lot of cheeses and meats, add some fresh fruit or berries to lighten up the board.
  5. Enjoy!
